Frequently Asked Questions

What even is BetterNYC?

We are a community-funded program that strives to lower the poverty rate by rewarding people who serve their community and are not in debt of any kind.

What are the benefits for signing up?

Those who are members and meet the requirements will be able to get exclusive discounts to participating stores and renters who participates will get a small percentate of commission back.
